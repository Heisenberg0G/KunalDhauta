About Me

Hola Folks!

Three years deep in the QA trenches. I break things on purpose because if it’s not broken in testing, it will be in production. Automation’s my go-to; manual testing catches what slips through. Quality over quantity. I break your code so users don’t have to, exposing bugs with surgical precision.

I’m passionate about building software that works flawlessly because behind every great user experience is quality assurance done right. Over the past three years, I’ve honed my skills in both automation and manual testing, tackling complex challenges and making sure no bug slips through the cracks. I believe that testing isn’t just a step in the process—it’s a mindset. When I’m not diving into code or breaking software to improve it, I’m constantly learning new tools and techniques to stay ahead of the curve. Clear communication, collaboration, and attention to detail are the pillars of my work ethic, and I’m always looking for smarter ways to make software more reliable and efficient. If you want someone who won’t just check boxes but will actively raise the bar for quality, let’s connect.
